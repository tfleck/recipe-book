Cake Batter Cake Bars★

  • 12 servings
  • 40 minutes
  • Luciana Vernola

Luciana’s recipe for cake batter cake bars

Ingredients

1 box yellow/vanilla cake mix
1/4 cup vegetable or canola oil
1 large egg
1/3 cup milk (or 1/2 cup)
1/2 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup rainbow sprinkles

Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit

Combine the cake mix, vegetable oil, egg, and milk. Make sure to add in the milk slowly. The batter will be very thick, this is what you want.

Mix in the chocolate chips and sprinkles

Bake for 25-30 mins or until the edges are light brown

Let cool for 30 mins before trying to cut, they’ll be too gooey

Enjoy!
